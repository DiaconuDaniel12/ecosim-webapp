ECO vs Eco Points

Two layers, one economy

ECO (Solana token)

ECO is the on-chain token powering the EcoSim ecosystem. It is designed for rewards and utility across the project. Trading and deeper on-chain features can be added as the game evolves.

  • On-chain token (Solana)
  • Rewards and ecosystem utility
  • Future integrations (marketplace, on-chain items)

Eco Points (in-game currency)

Eco Points are used inside the game for items, upgrades, and progression. They stay fast and frictionless for gameplay. Eco Points are designed for balance, not speculation.

  • In-game currency
  • Items, upgrades, progression
  • Balance and gameplay-first design

Reinvestment model

EcoSim reinvests ecosystem revenue into growing the game: development, new content, infrastructure, security, marketing, and operations (including salaries). The priority is improving the player experience and the in-game economy.
