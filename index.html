Decentralized Economy

Three pillars: Commercial, Governor, Guild. Each adds demand, regulates flow, and connects players.

Commercial

Shops, farms, workshops producing resources and goods; create core demand.

Governor

Taxes, licenses, balancing. Adjust difficulty, rewards, and pace of progression.

Guild

Social hubs, quests, local markets. Coordinate supply chains and sharing.

ECO Flow

ECO enters via rewards/purchases, exits via upgrades, merges, and fees.

Assets & Merges

Workers, buildings, and tools are assets. Fusion (merge) raises rarity and reduces supply.

Star Tiers

Combine assets to climb tiers. Higher output, lower supply as low-tier units are burned.

Deflation Engine

Every merge removes items from circulation, tightening supply and improving value.

Marketplace

In-Game

On-Chain (Later)
